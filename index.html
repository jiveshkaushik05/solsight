What is SolSight?

A Web3-powered accessibility platform where smart glasses live‑stream a user's surroundings to global volunteers who assist in real time and earn on‑chain reputation and rewards.

Features

1) Smart glasses capture

Frames are streamed securely from the user’s perspective.

2) Stream to volunteers

The video is sent to vetted volunteers prepared to help.

3) Assist and earn

Helpers earn on‑chain reputation points and rewards.
